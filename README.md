# Task 2: Customer Segmentation Using K-Means

## Objective
Segment customers based on income and spending behavior to improve marketing strategies.

## Approach
- Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Applied K-Means clustering
- Used Elbow Method to find optimal clusters
- Visualized clusters using scatter plots

## Results
- 5 customer segments identified

## Key Insights
- High income & high spending customers are most valuable
- High income & low spending customers are potential targets
- Low income & high spending customers show impulsive behavior

## Business Value
- Enables targeted marketing
- Improves customer engagement
- Optimizes business strategies

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Scikit-learn
